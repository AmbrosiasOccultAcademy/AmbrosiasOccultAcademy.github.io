Tarot Tuesday

Week of June 18 – 25

Each week, we step through the veil with a featured tarot archetype, exploring its lessons, rituals, and reflections for the days ahead. This week, the currents are ruled by The High Priestess.

The High Priestess – Guardian of the Inner Temple

The High Priestess sits between worlds—between conscious and subconscious, seen and unseen, logic and mystery. She doesn’t shout; she waits. Her power is not in action, but in awareness. When she appears as our guide for the week, she invites us to slow down and listen to what has been whispering beneath the noise.

Keywords

  • Intuition
  • Mystery
  • Inner knowing
  • Hidden truths
  • Spiritual insight

Upright Themes

  • Trusting your instincts
  • Receiving subtle signs and synchronicities
  • Honoring your emotional tides
  • Letting answers emerge instead of forcing them

Shadow / Reversed Themes

  • Ignoring your inner voice
  • Overthinking and second‑guessing
  • Secrets, half‑truths, or withheld information
  • Spiritual burnout or disconnection

This Week’s Energy: June 18 – 25

Expect a quieter, more liminal tone to the week. Answers may arrive through dreams, déjà vu, or sudden “knowing” that can’t be logically explained. This is not a time to push outcomes or demand clarity on your schedule. Instead, it’s a time to cultivate trust in your own inner compass.

Tuesday – Opening the Veil

June 18

Set the tone for the week by creating a small sacred space: a candle, a glass of water, your deck, and your High Priestess card. Ask: “What wants to be revealed to me this week?” Pull one clarifying card and write down the first three words that come to mind.

Wednesday – Listening Between the Lines

June 19

Pay attention to conversations, lyrics, and passing comments. The High Priestess often speaks through subtle echoes. Notice what repeats today. Journal on: “What pattern is trying to get my attention?”

Thursday – Dreamgate

June 20

Before sleep, place your High Priestess card by your bed. Ask for a dream message. Upon waking, write down any images, feelings, or fragments—no matter how strange. The meaning may unfold over the next few days.

Friday – Sacred “No”

June 21

The High Priestess teaches boundaries through intuition. Today, practice saying a gentle but firm “no” to anything that feels off, draining, or misaligned—even if you can’t fully explain why.

Saturday – Quiet Ritual

June 22

Choose a simple ritual: a bath, a walk at dusk, or silent candle gazing. Leave your phone behind. Let your mind wander and see what rises when you’re not distracting yourself.

Sunday – Inner Oracle Spread

June 23

Try this 3‑card spread: (1) What my intuition is already telling me, (2) What my mind is afraid of, (3) How to honor my inner knowing. Place the High Priestess above the spread as the overseeing guide.

Monday – Integration

June 24–25

Review your notes, dreams, and spreads from the week. Ask: “What have I learned about the way my intuition speaks?” Write a short “Intuition Manifesto” for yourself—three promises about how you’ll listen to your inner voice moving forward.

3‑Minute Intuition Check‑In

Use this simple practice any day this week when you feel scattered or unsure:

  1. Be still: Sit comfortably, close your eyes, and take three slow breaths.
  2. Ask: “What does my inner voice want me to know right now?”
  3. Receive: Write down the very first sentence, image, or phrase that arises—no editing.

Over time, you’ll start to recognize the unique “tone” of your intuition versus fear, habit, or outside influence.
