The Hand Guide

Explore the ancient art of palmistry — the study of lines, mounts, and shapes that reveal personality and destiny.

Major Lines

  • Life Line: Vitality, health, and life path.
  • Heart Line: Emotional depth and relationships.
  • Head Line: Intellect, creativity, and decision‑making.
  • Fate Line: Career, destiny, and life direction.

Mounts & Fingers

Each mount corresponds to a planet — Venus, Jupiter, Saturn, Apollo, and Mercury — revealing traits and tendencies. Finger shapes and lengths add nuance to interpretation.

Hand Shapes

Earth, Air, Fire, and Water hands reflect elemental balance and personality archetypes.

Palm Feature of the Week

The Mystic Cross — a rare marking between the Head and Heart lines, symbolizing intuitive wisdom and spiritual insight.
